Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

What we collect

When you sign up for Relay, we collect your name, .edu email address, skills you list, and session activity (who you taught and learned from). We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or location data beyond what's implied by your .edu domain.

How we use it

Your data powers the Relay marketplace — matching teachers with learners, tracking credits, and showing profiles. We never sell your data to third parties. We may use anonymized aggregate data for research and improvement.

Data retention

Your account and data remain until you request deletion. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us. When deleted, your profile and personal data are removed within 30 days. Session records may be kept anonymized for platform analytics.

Cookies

Relay uses a single cookie to keep you logged in. It's HTTP-only, same-site restricted, and contains no personal data — just a session identifier. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party cookies.

Security

Passwords are hashed (not stored in plain text). All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. We rate-limit login attempts to prevent brute-force attacks. Your data is stored in a secure database with no public network access.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at at5441@nyu.edu.

⚖️ Your rights. You can request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or delete your account at any time. Relay is a student project and we take your privacy seriously.
