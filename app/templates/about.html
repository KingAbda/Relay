About Relay

Trading skills, not money.

A peer-to-peer skill exchange built by students who got tired of doing free labor.

💡 The Idea

What if there was a structured way for students to trade skills — no cash, just time? That's Relay. 30 minutes of teaching = 1 credit. Spend credits to learn anything. Verified through your .edu email.

📊 The Data

300+ student interviews confirmed: 70% have a skill they want compensation for, 85% want to learn but can't afford it, 75% supply-demand match rate.

🎯 The Mission

Turn campus favor culture into fair culture. Every student has something to teach and something to learn. Relay connects them without the awkwardness of pricing your knowledge.

🔥 The Vision

Start at NYU, expand campus by campus. A world where every student has access to any skill they want to learn — paid forward, not paid for.

AT

Abdurrahman Touray — Founder

First-generation Gambian American, NYU 2026 (HEOP Scholar). Relay started in Abu Dhabi — a friend taught me music production, I helped him start going to the gym. No money changed hands. That moment sparked a question: what if there was a system for this?

After 300+ student interviews and a 50-person pilot, Relay is built for students who want to learn without breaking the bank and teach without being undervalued.
