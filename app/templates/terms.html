Terms of Service

Last updated: June 8, 2026

1. What Relay Is

Relay is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform for university students. Users trade skills using a time-based credit system (30 minutes = 1 credit). No real money changes hands between users for individual sessions. Relay is currently a student project and provided "as is" without warranty.

2. Eligibility

You must be a current college or university student with a valid .edu email address to use Relay. You must be at least 18 years old. By using Relay, you represent that you meet these requirements.

3. User Conduct

You agree to:

  • Treat other users with respect — no harassment, discrimination, or abuse
  • Show up to scheduled sessions or cancel with reasonable notice
  • Teach skills you genuinely know — don't misrepresent your abilities
  • Not use Relay for any illegal activity or to share prohibited content
  • Not create multiple accounts or attempt to game the credit system

4. Credits

Credits are not real currency and have no cash value. They cannot be bought, sold, transferred outside the platform, or redeemed for money. Credits are earned through teaching and can be spent on learning. Relay reserves the right to adjust credit balances in cases of abuse or system error.

5. Disclaimers

Relay is a platform that connects users — we do not verify the accuracy of skills listed, the quality of teaching, or the safety of in-person meetings. Users participate at their own risk. We recommend meeting in public campus spaces for sessions. Relay is not responsible for disputes between users.

6. Content

You retain ownership of the content you post (profile info, skills, descriptions). By posting, you grant Relay a license to display this content on the platform. We may remove content that violates these terms.

7. Termination

We may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us.
