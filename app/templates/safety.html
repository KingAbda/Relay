Last updated: June 17, 2026

3. Release of Liability

4. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify Relay from claims arising from your participation or violation of terms.

5. Medical Authorization

You authorize Relay to seek emergency medical treatment on your behalf if you are unable to do so.

6. Safety Guidelines

  • Meet in public campus spaces only
  • Tell someone where you are going
  • Warm up and assess the space
  • Know your limits — stop if unsafe
  • No medical or clinical skills
  • No alcohol or drugs
  • Report unsafe behavior to at5441@nyu.edu

Nothing on Relay constitutes medical advice. Consult a healthcare provider before any physical program.

All users are independent participants, not employees or agents of Relay.
